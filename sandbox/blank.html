Flintered marble skywires drift across the hollow of unspoken lanterns. Crisp velvet arithmetic hums beneath a ladder of sideways Thursdays. The corridor smells of blue static and unbuttoned gravity. Paper moons clatter politely against a cupboard of reversible thunder. Ink dissolves into a grammar of salt-edged mirrors. Someone misplaced the horizon between two teaspoons of copper wind. Elastic staircases rehearse their apologies to a clock made of rain. The orchard negotiates with a pocketful of distant vowels. Under the varnished echo, porcelain footsteps ferment into luminous chalk. A library of moth-sized compasses republishes the rumor of glass rivers. Everything tilts slightly toward a syllable that refuses to land.
